Fatigue / Weakness

Fatigue is a feeling of tiredness or lack of energy. It can be temporary due to lifestyle factors or a sign of an underlying health condition.

Possible Causes:

  • Poor sleep or irregular sleep schedule
  • Anemia or nutritional deficiencies
  • Chronic stress or depression
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Infections or chronic illnesses

General Remedies:

  • Maintain a regular sleep routine
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in iron and vitamins
  • Stay hydrated
  • Exercise regularly to boost energy
  • Practice stress management techniques (meditation, yoga)

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Persistent fatigue lasting more than 2 weeks
  • Fatigue with unexplained weight loss
  • Weakness interfering with daily activities
  • Fatigue with chest pain, shortness of breath, or dizziness
