Dizziness

Dizziness is a feeling of lightheadedness, unsteadiness, or loss of balance. It can be temporary or a sign of an underlying condition.

Possible Causes:

  • Dehydration or low blood pressure
  • Inner ear problems (vertigo, labyrinthitis)
  • Low blood sugar
  • Anemia or vitamin deficiencies
  • Anxiety or panic attacks

General Remedies:

  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Rest in a safe position
  • Eat small, frequent meals to maintain blood sugar
  • Practice balance exercises (for vertigo)
  • Ginger tea or peppermint tea

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Frequent or severe dizziness
  • Dizziness with fainting or chest pain
  • Sudden dizziness with vision changes or weakness
  • Persistent dizziness interfering with daily life
