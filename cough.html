Cough

Cough is a reflex to clear your airways. It can be caused by infections, allergies, or irritants.

Possible Causes:

  • Common cold or flu
  • Allergies or asthma
  • Respiratory infections
  • Smoking or pollution

General Remedies:

  • Honey with warm water
  • Ginger tea or turmeric milk
  • Steam inhalation
  • Saltwater gargle

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Cough lasting more than 2–3 weeks
  • Blood in sputum
  • Chest pain or breathlessness
  • High fever with cough
