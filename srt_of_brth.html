Shortness of Breath

Shortness of breath (dyspnea) can be a sign of respiratory or heart-related issues. It may occur suddenly or develop gradually.

Possible Causes:

  • Asthma or COPD
  • Heart disease (heart failure, angina)
  • Lung infections (pneumonia, bronchitis)
  • Anxiety or panic attacks
  • Low physical fitness or obesity

General Remedies:

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Stay physically active and maintain fitness
  • Avoid smoking and polluted environments
  • Use prescribed inhalers (for asthma/COPD)
  • Maintain hydration

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Sudden severe breathlessness
  • Chest pain with shortness of breath
  • Blue lips or fingertips
  • Difficulty speaking or confusion
