Chest Pain

Chest pain can be caused by heart, lung, or digestive problems. It is important to identify the underlying reason.

Possible Causes:

  • Heart disease (angina, heart attack)
  • Gastroesophageal reflux (GERD)
  • Lung infection (pneumonia, pleurisy)
  • Muscle strain or injury

General Remedies:

  • Rest and relaxation
  • Avoid spicy/oily food (for GERD)
  • Warm compress for muscle strain
  • Breathing exercises for mild lung issues

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Sudden severe chest pain
  • Pain spreading to arm, jaw, or back
  • Chest pain with sweating, nausea, or breathlessness
  • Persistent pain not improving with rest
