Stomach Pain

Stomach pain can range from mild indigestion to serious medical conditions. Understanding the cause is important for proper care.

Possible Causes:

  • Indigestion or overeating
  • Gastritis or acid reflux
  • Food poisoning or infection
  • Constipation or irritable bowel syndrome (IBS)

General Remedies:

  • Drink warm water or herbal teas (ginger, peppermint)
  • Follow BRAT diet (Banana, Rice, Applesauce, Toast)
  • Apply warm compress to stomach
  • Rest and avoid spicy/oily food

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Severe or persistent pain
  • Blood in stool or vomit
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Pain with fever or vomiting
