Skin Rash

Skin rashes are changes in skin color or texture, often caused by allergies, infections, or chronic conditions.

Possible Causes:

  • Allergic reactions (food, medication, cosmetics)
  • Eczema or dermatitis
  • Fungal infections
  • Heat rash or insect bites

General Remedies:

  • Apply moisturizers to keep skin hydrated
  • Use cool compress to reduce itching
  • Antihistamines for allergic rashes (doctor’s advice)
  • Keep affected area clean and dry

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Rash spreading rapidly
  • Rash with fever or pus
  • Rash affecting eyes, mouth, or genitals
  • Persistent rash not improving with home care
