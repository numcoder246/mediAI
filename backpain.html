Back Pain

Back pain is a common issue that can result from poor posture, muscle strain, or underlying medical conditions.

Possible Causes:

  • Poor posture or long sitting hours
  • Muscle strain or injury
  • Herniated disc
  • Arthritis or osteoporosis

General Remedies:

  • Apply heat or ice packs
  • Gentle stretching and yoga
  • Maintain proper posture
  • Use ergonomic chairs and mattresses
  • Regular exercise and weight management

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Back pain with numbness or weakness
  • Pain spreading to legs
  • Difficulty controlling bladder or bowel
  • Persistent pain not improving with rest
