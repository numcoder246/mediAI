Fever

Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, often due to an infection. It is a common symptom that indicates your body is fighting against illness.

Possible Causes:

  • Viral infections (Flu, COVID-19)
  • Bacterial infections (Typhoid, Pneumonia)
  • Inflammatory conditions
  • Heat exhaustion

General Remedies:

  • Stay hydrated (water, clear soups, ORS)
  • Rest and sleep
  • Light clothing and cool environment
  • Paracetamol (only as per doctor’s advice)
  • Cold compress on forehead

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Fever above 102°F (38.9°C)
  • Fever lasting more than 3 days
  • Severe headache, chest pain, or breathing difficulty
  • Rash, confusion, or persistent vomiting
