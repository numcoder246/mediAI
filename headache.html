Headache

Headaches are one of the most common health complaints. They can be caused by stress, dehydration, vision strain, or underlying medical conditions.

Possible Causes:

  • Stress or tension
  • Migraine
  • Eye strain or poor posture
  • Dehydration
  • Sinus infection

General Remedies:

  • Stay hydrated
  • Cold or warm compress on forehead/neck
  • Proper rest and sleep
  • Massage temples or relaxation techniques
  • Limit screen time and correct posture

When to Seek Medical Help:

  • Severe headache with nausea or vomiting
  • Headache lasting more than a few days
  • Sudden intense headache (“worst headache ever”)
  • Headache with vision changes or weakness
